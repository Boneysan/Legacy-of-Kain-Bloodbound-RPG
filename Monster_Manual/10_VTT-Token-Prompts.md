# VTT & Bestiary Image Generation Guide

This document provides optimized prompts for AI image generators (Grok-2, Midjourney, DALL-E 3, etc.) to create visually consistent assets for *Legacy of Kain: Bloodbound RPG*.

---

## 1. Style Bases

### **Style A: Tactical VTT Token**
> **Token Base:** *Dark gothic fantasy, tactical VTT token, 45-degree top-down isometric view, clean circular composition, cinematic rim lighting, desaturated colors with glowing highlights, isolated on a solid dark neutral grey background. High-contrast textures.*

### **Style B: Bestiary Illustration**
> **Bestiary Base:** *Full-body professional RPG bestiary illustration, dark gothic fantasy, oil painting style with fine digital detail, dramatic chiaroscuro lighting, moody atmospheric background, intricate armor and skin textures, 8k resolution, inspired by Legacy of Kain concept art.*

## 3. Technical Specs
1.  **Aspect Ratio (Tokens):** Use `--ar 1:1`.
2.  **Aspect Ratio (Bestiary):** Use `--ar 2:3` or `--ar 3:4` (Portrait).
3.  **Refining:** Add `--no saturation, bright colors` for a grittier look.
4.  **Generative Consistency:** Use the exact `Style Base` text to maintain cohesion across all tokens and illustrations.
