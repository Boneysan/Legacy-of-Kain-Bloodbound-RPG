# Chapter 6: Skill Checks and Abilities

In the shadowed ruins of Nosgoth, every action carries weight. The skill check system governs how characters resolve tasks, face challenges, and shape the narrative. This chapter details the consolidated 20-skill system, featuring **Active Utilities** that provide tactical options in combat and high-stakes scenes.

## 6.1 Skill Check Mechanics

Skill checks use a D6 dice pool: **Attribute + Skill Level**. Each 5 or 6 counts as one success.

### 6.1.1 Difficulty Ratings (DR)
| DR | Difficulty | Successes | Example |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Simple | 1 | Jumping a small gap; basic first aid |
| 2 | Standard | 2 | Picking a common lock; standard attack |
| 3 | Challenging | 3 | Disarming a complex trap; resisting a potent glyph |
| 4 | Difficult | 4 | Casting under extreme pressure; navigating cursed fog |
| 5+ | Legendary | 5+ | Breaking chains of prophecy; possessing a boss |

## 6.2 The Consolidated Skill List

Skills are grouped into five categories. Each skill now features an **Active Utility**—a specific action or bonus you can trigger to gain a tactical advantage.

### 6.2.1 Combat Skills
| Skill | Attributes | Active Utility (The "Action") |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Weapon Mastery** | Fury or Shadow | **Precision Strike:** Spend a Bonus Action to ignore 1 point of Armor on your next hit. |
| **Evasion** | Shadow or Focus | **Riposte:** If an enemy misses you by 2+ successes, you gain Advantage on your next attack against them. |
| **Unarmed Combat**| Blood or Fury | **Grapple/Throw:** Use an Action to force a DR 2 Blood save or the target is *Restrained* or *Prone*. |
| **Athletics** | Fury or Blood | **Pounce/Vault:** Move an extra 10 ft as part of an attack; deal +1 damage if you moved 20+ ft. |
| **Tactics** | Focus or Will | **Command:** Use an Action to grant an ally an immediate Move action or **+2 dice** on their next roll. |

### 6.2.2 Arcane & Spectral Skills
| Skill | Attributes | Active Utility (The "Action") |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Glyphcasting** | Soul or Will | **Overcharge:** Increase the radius of a Glyph by 5 ft or its damage by 1. |
| **Ritualism** | Soul or Blood | **Sanguine Rite:** Sacrifice 2 HP to gain +2 dice to a Ritual check. |
| **Ethereal Mastery**| Soul or Shadow | **Phase Control:** Use an Action to grant the party **Resistance** to Spectral damage (halving Soul Bleed and spectral hazards) for 1 minute (once per scene). |
| **Forbidden Knowledge**| Soul or Focus | **Weakness Analysis:** Use an Action to reveal a Boss's "Vulnerability" or "A.I. Tag". |
| **Concentration** | Will or Blood | **Iron Mind:** Spend a Bonus Action to ignore one "Shaken" or "Fear" effect for 1 round. |

### 6.2.3 Subterfuge Skills
| Skill | Attributes | Active Utility (The "Action") |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stealth** | Shadow or Focus | **Vanish:** If in dim light, use an Action to re-enter Stealth even if previously seen. |
| **Infiltration** | Shadow or Focus | **Sabotage:** Disable a non-magical trap or mechanism as a Bonus Action. |
| **Observation** | Focus or Shadow | **Expose:** Use an Action to reveal a hidden enemy's exact HP and Defense Value (DV). |
| **Blood Sense** | Blood or Soul | **Vampiric Hunt:** Passive: +1 die to hit Bleeding targets. Active: Sense all living in 60 ft. |

### 6.2.4 Influence Skills
| Skill | Attributes | Active Utility (The "Action") |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Persuasion** | Will or Soul | **Parley:** Use an Action to delay a non-mindless enemy’s turn to the end of the round. |
| **Intimidation** | Fury or Blood | **Terrify:** Force a DR 2 Will save or the enemy suffers Disadvantage on their next attack. |
| **Insight** | Will or Focus | **Cold Read:** Bonus Action to learn an NPC’s "Motivation" or "Secret Goal." |

### 6.2.5 Expertise Skills
| Skill | Attributes | Active Utility (The "Action") |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Lore** | Focus or Will | **Tactical Archive:** Use an Action to grant the party +1 Armor against a specific enemy type for the scene. |
| **Wilderness** | Focus or Blood | **Field Medic:** Use an Action to heal an ally for 2 HP. A creature can only benefit from this skill utility once per scene. |
| **Craft** | Focus or Soul | **Emergency Repair:** Use an Action to restore 1 point of Armor to a piece of gear or fix a "Jammed" weapon. |

## 6.3 Pushing and Stunts

- **Pushing a Roll:** Add 1–3 Corruption Dice to a failed roll. 5–6 is a success; 1 increases Corruption.
- **Stunts:** A roll that exceeds its target (DR or DV) by 2 or more successes allows for a **Stunt**. Extra successes can be spent to add damage, inflict conditions, or gain tactical advantages as described in Chapter 9.

## 6.4 Skill Point Economy

- **Starting Points:** Characters begin with **5–6 skill points** (based on class) at Level 1.
- **Skill Cap:** Maximum of 3 ranks at Level 1; maximum of 5 ranks at Level 20.
- **Progression:** Gain **1 skill point** per level. 
- **Active Mastery:** When you reach 4 ranks in a skill, you may use its Active Utility as a Bonus Action (if it was an Action) or twice per scene (if it was once per scene).
