# Chapter 6: Skill Checks and Abilities

In the shadowed ruins of Nosgoth, every action carries weight. The skill check system governs how characters resolve tasks, face challenges, and shape the narrative. This chapter details the consolidated 20-skill system, featuring **Active Utilities** that provide tactical options in combat and high-stakes scenes.

## 6.1 Skill Check Mechanics

Skill checks use a D6 dice pool: **Attribute + Skill Level**. Each 5 or 6 counts as one success.

### 6.1.1 Difficulty Ratings (DR)
| DR | Difficulty | Successes | Example |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Simple | 1 | Jumping a small gap; basic first aid |
| 2 | Standard | 2 | Picking a common lock; climbing a rope under pressure |
| 3 | Challenging | 3 | Disarming a complex trap; resisting a potent glyph |
| 4 | Difficult | 4 | Casting under extreme pressure; navigating cursed fog |
| 5+ | Legendary | 5+ | Breaking chains of prophecy; possessing a boss |

> **Note:** Combat attacks are resolved against a target's **DV (Defense Value)**, not a DR. DV is determined by the defender's attributes and is listed on their stat block or character sheet. DR is used for skill checks, saves, and non-combat ability effects.

## 6.2 The Consolidated Skill List

Skills are grouped into five categories. Each skill now features an **Active Utility**—a specific action or bonus you can trigger to gain a tactical advantage.

### 6.2.1 Combat Skills
| Skill | Attributes | Active Utility (The "Action") |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Weapon Mastery** | Fury or Shadow | **Precision Strike:** Spend a Bonus Action to ignore 1 point of Armor on your next hit. |
| **Evasion** | Shadow or Focus | **Riposte:** If an attacker's roll falls 2 or more successes short of your DV (i.e., they miss by a wide margin), you gain Advantage on your next attack against that attacker before the end of your next turn. |
| **Unarmed Combat**| Blood or Fury | **Grapple/Throw:** Use an Action to force a DR 2 Blood save (range: adjacent). On failure, the target is **Restrained** or **Prone** (your choice). |
| **Athletics** | Fury or Blood | **Pounce/Vault:** Move an extra 10 ft as part of an attack; deal +1 damage if you moved 20+ ft. |
| **Tactics** | Focus or Will | **Command:** Use an Action to grant an ally an immediate Move action or **+2 dice** on their next roll. |

### 6.2.2 Arcane & Spectral Skills
| Skill | Attributes | Active Utility (The "Action") |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Glyphcasting** | Soul or Will | **Overcharge:** Increase the radius of a Glyph by 5 ft or its damage by 1. |
| **Ritualism** | Soul or Blood | **Sanguine Rite:** Sacrifice 2 HP to gain +2 dice to a Ritual check. |
| **Ethereal Mastery**| Soul or Shadow | **Phase Control:** Use an Action to grant the party **Resistance** to Spectral damage (halving Soul Bleed and spectral hazards) for 1 minute (once per encounter). |
| **Forbidden Knowledge**| Soul or Focus | **Weakness Analysis:** Use an Action to reveal a Boss's "Vulnerability" or "A.I. Tag". |
| **Concentration** | Will or Blood | **Iron Mind:** Spend a Bonus Action to ignore one **Shaken** or **Frightened** effect for 1 round. |

### 6.2.3 Subterfuge Skills
| Skill | Attributes | Active Utility (The "Action") |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stealth** | Shadow or Focus | **Vanish:** If in dim light, use an Action to re-enter Stealth even if previously seen. |
| **Infiltration** | Shadow or Focus | **Sabotage:** Disable a non-magical trap or mechanism as a Bonus Action. |
| **Observation** | Focus or Shadow | **Expose:** Use an Action to reveal a hidden enemy's exact HP and Defense Value (DV). |
| **Blood Sense** | Blood or Soul | **Vampiric Hunt:** Passive: +1 die on attack rolls against **Bleeding** targets (always active). Active: Use an Action to sense the presence and rough direction of all living creatures within 60 feet until the start of your next turn. |

### 6.2.4 Influence Skills
| Skill | Attributes | Active Utility (The "Action") |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Persuasion** | Will or Soul | **Parley:** Use an Action (range: within 30 feet; target must be able to hear or understand you) to delay one non-mindless enemy. That enemy's initiative is moved to the end of the current round. It still takes its full turn when it acts; this utility only changes when, not whether, it acts. Mindless creatures (Skeletons, constructs without direction) are immune. |
| **Intimidation** | Fury or Blood | **Terrify:** Use an Action (range: within 30 feet; target must be able to perceive you) to force one enemy to make a DR 2 Will save. On failure, the target suffers **Disadvantage** on their next attack roll before the end of their next turn. |
| **Insight** | Will or Focus | **Cold Read:** Bonus Action to learn an NPC’s "Motivation" or "Secret Goal." |

### 6.2.5 Expertise Skills
| Skill | Attributes | Active Utility (The "Action") |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Lore** | Focus or Will | **Tactical Archive:** Use an Action to grant the party +1 Armor against one specific creature type (e.g., Undead, Mortals, Beasts, Constructs — use Monster Manual chapter groupings) for the remainder of the encounter (once per encounter). This bonus is a flat addition to each character's existing Armor score and does not stack with itself. |
| **Wilderness** | Focus or Blood | **Field Medic:** Use an Action (range: adjacent or within 5 feet) to heal one ally for 2 HP. A creature can only benefit from this skill utility once per encounter. |
| **Craft** | Focus or Soul | **Emergency Repair:** Use an Action (range: Touch) to restore 1 point of Armor to a piece of gear, or to clear a **Jammed** weapon (a weapon that misfired, fouled, or was disabled by a trap or environmental effect, rendering it unusable until repaired). |

## 6.3 Pushing and Stunts

- **Pushing a Roll:** After seeing the result of a failed roll, you may Push it by adding 1, 2, or 3 Corruption Dice to your pool. Resolve each Corruption Die: a **5–6** adds 1 success to the original roll; a **1** increases your Corruption Level by 1; a **2–4** has no additional effect. You declare the Push before the GM describes the failure. For full rules, see Chapter 8 §8.2.
- **Stunts:** When a roll produces more successes than required to meet the DR or DV, each extra success can be spent to add damage, inflict conditions, or gain tactical advantages as described in Chapter 9 §9.6. The GM may require at least 1 extra success for a minor stunt and at least 2 extra successes for a dramatic or scene-altering stunt, at their discretion.

## 6.4 Skill Point Economy

- **Starting Points:** Characters begin with **5–6 skill points** (based on class) at Level 1.
- **Skill Cap:** The maximum rank in any single skill is limited by character level as follows:
  - Levels 1–4: Maximum rank **3**
  - Levels 5–9: Maximum rank **4**
  - Level 10+: Maximum rank **5**
- **Progression:** Gain **1 skill point** per level.
- **Active Mastery:** When you reach 4 ranks in a skill, that skill's Active Utility improves:
  - If the utility required an **Action**, it now requires only a **Bonus Action**.
  - If the utility was already a **Bonus Action**, it may now be used **twice per encounter** instead of once.
  - If the utility was usable **once per encounter**, it may now be used **twice per encounter**.
