Nosgoth's Last Twilight
Genre: Dark Fantasy / Gothic Horror
 Inspired by: Legacy of Kain series, Divinity: Original Sin 2, Dark Souls, Bloodborne

📜 Setting
Nosgoth is a dying land. Once ruled by the immortal vampire Kain and balanced by the ancient Pillars, it is now a realm torn by decay, divine silence, and civil war. Kain has vanished. In his absence, vampire clans wage bloody feuds, the Sarafan Order ignites a holy war against all supernatural forces, and eldritch cults whisper of the end of days.
Among this chaos, a prophecy emerges: the Abyssal Heart, an artifact said to restore Nosgoth—or destroy it. Some claim it is the source of Kain’s immortality, others, a prison for a forgotten god. Its shards are scattered across Nosgoth, hidden, protected, or corrupted by powerful factions. To possess the Heart is to decide the fate of the world.

🎭 Player Role
Each character is bound to this prophecy—by curse, vision, vengeance, or fate. Drawn from diverse origins (vampire, mortal, revenant, unbound), they are marked by the same mysterious power. Their journey will force them to navigate personal darkness, rival factions, and the crumbling veil between the Material and Spectral Realms.

🖤 The Abyssal Heart
A legendary relic that echoes through prophecy and madness.
Believed to be tied to Kain’s power—or the end of the world


Broken into shards hidden across Nosgoth


Each shard is a trial: spiritual, moral, or apocalyptic


Reassembling it grants a wish, a curse, or something older still



🔥 Core Themes
Fate vs. Free Will – Are the characters merely players in a script written by gods?


Corruption and Redemption – Can rot be reversed? Is power always damning?


Power and the Price It Demands – What are you willing to sacrifice to win?


Death, Undeath, and the Nature of the Soul – What defines a soul? Who owns it?


The Cyclical Nature of War and Rule – Will you repeat the past—or break it?



🏰 Major Factions
Sarafan Order – Fanatical knight-priests, hellbent on purging Nosgoth of vampires and heresy.
 Scions of Kain – Fractured vampire clans seeking to seize power, reform Kain’s empire, or destroy their rivals.
 Cult of the Forgotten Ones – Worshippers of ancient pre-vampiric gods who believe reality must be undone to be reborn.
 Nosgothian Outcasts – Mercenaries, nomads, heretics, and revenants trying to survive without aligning with any great power.

🧭 The Journey
The party’s quest will span the ruins of Avernus, the haunted forest of Vampyr Grove, the shifting shadows of the Spectral Realm, and ancient battlegrounds where gods once bled. They will face betrayals, rewrite prophecies, and ultimately decide whether Nosgoth deserves to be saved—or reforged.
Choices will twist the land. Victory may feel like ruin. And power always comes at a cost.

🎭 Tone & Style
Tragic. Mythic. Violent. Mysterious.
 A gothic opera of fading gods, cursed immortals, and dying lights.
 Every decision reshapes the story. Every success brings the shadow closer.
