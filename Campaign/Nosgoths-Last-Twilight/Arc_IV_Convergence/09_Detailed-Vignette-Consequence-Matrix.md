## 03 — What Reth Wants

| Choice | NPC shift | State and pressure guidance | Encounter handoff |
|--------|-----------|-----------------------------|-------------------|
| A — Offer Structure | Reth names the coalition's accountability gap and forces the party to admit that structure alone is not legitimacy | Set `coalition_shape: structure-challenged`. This is not a failure state, but it guarantees the Fulcrum examination will ask who the coalition is actually for. | Carry this directly into Vignette 04. If the route or chamber gets pressured, the weakest point should be the coalition's answer to exclusion, not just force allocation. |
| B — Offer a Seat in the Decision | If the mechanism is real, Reth becomes neutral rather than aligned; if the mechanism is vague, the branch collapses and the party have effectively promised what they cannot execute | Set `coalition_shape: procession-neutral` only if the party name a concrete method. Otherwise, do not award the result. The Procession is practical about false inclusion. | A valid neutral result removes one major logistics complication from Encounters/Arc_IV_Convergence/03_Road-to-the-Hall.md and the final execution scene. |
| C — Ask What Reth Would Do | Reth becomes a legitimate policy counter-voice instead of only a catastrophe prophet; the Wheel loses exclusive claim to moral seriousness | Set `wheel_final_state: contested` and `hylden_gate_resolution: reth-adjacent` where appropriate. This creates a real alternative that can be cited in the Hall even if the party reject it. | Feed this into Vignette 05 if the party want to name public-consent conditions or exclusion logic on the record. |
| D — Name What the Procession Has Cost | Reth names the party's unresolved grief back to them and turns that grief into an explicit strategic variable | Set `coalition_shape: grief-named`. The important consequence is not agreement with Reth; it is that the coalition now has a named wound it can either hide or use honestly later. | Carry the named grief into Vignette 04 and especially Vignette 05. If a final declaration is honest about cost, this branch makes that honesty more legible. |
