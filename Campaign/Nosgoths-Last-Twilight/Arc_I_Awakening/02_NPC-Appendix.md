# Arc I NPC Appendix

Use these as the recurring cast for Awakening. Replace or retire any of them if the party kills, converts, exposes, or abandons them.

## Serit Vale

- Role: Pale Accord courier and reluctant field operative
- Public face: Controlled scholar carrying sealed research under emergency authority
- Hidden truth: She already suspects the Sanguine Knot responds to inherited blood and has concealed how much the Accord knows about the Blood-Well
- Wants from the party: Protection, access, and enough trust to keep control of the artifact conversation
- Best use: The first helpful liar in the campaign; useful, intelligent, and impossible to trust fully
- If lost: Replace with another Accord agent carrying Serit's notes and a narrower moral horizon

## Mera Ash

- Role: Ashwalker outrider and Guild survival specialist
- Public face: Practical caravan hand who values routes, quiet, and payment over ideology
- Hidden truth: She is already calculating which version of events is most profitable to the Guilds
- Wants from the party: A survivable road out, clear decisions, and no grand speeches while people are dying
- Best use: The voice of logistics, timing, and ordinary survival under supernatural pressure
- If lost: Replace with another Guild operative, but keep the tone practical rather than mystical

## Brother Halden

- Role: Roadside confessor, hospice keeper, or charitable steward near Avernus
- Public face: Soft-spoken caretaker offering shelter and ritual calm
- Hidden truth: He is compromised, observed, blackmailed, or spiritually entangled with a Hash'ak'gik cell even if he is not a full cultist yet
- Wants from the party: To survive exposure and avoid becoming the first obvious villain in the room
- Best use: The false refuge made human; kind enough to be dangerous
- If lost: Promote his records, prayers, or hidden correspondence into the clue trail

## Sister Caris

- Role: Healer and relief organizer inside Avernus precincts
- Public face: Tireless caregiver who keeps triage functioning when everyone else is panicking
- Hidden truth: She knows more about which patients, pilgrims, and clergy are compromised than she admits, because naming them openly would collapse the refuge
- Wants from the party: Discretion, protection for the innocent, and proof before accusation
- Best use: The good person caught inside a system already contaminated
- If lost: Make her death or disappearance a sign that public safety in Avernus has failed

## Confessor Ilyr Voss

- Role: Sarafan advance investigator
- Public face: Branded lay confessor or scout speaking in the language of procedure rather than immediate slaughter
- Hidden truth: He would prefer a clean case over a massacre, but his reports still feed the purge machine
- Wants from the party: Confession, evidence, or a mistake large enough to justify intervention
- Best use: Early Sarafan pressure before open crusade arrives
- If lost: Escalate straight to harder-line Sarafan response

## Sister Nerys Vale

- Role: Wandering Warden of Balance or lay adjudicator attached to no court long enough to be owned by it
- Public face: Spare, calm, and difficult to impress, speaking as if every argument has a cost beyond the room it happens in
- Hidden truth: She is already tracking the Heart's pressure and fears the party may become necessary long before they become trustworthy
- Wants from the party: Evidence that they can bear power without calling appetite judgment
- Best use: Seed Balance as a living philosophy before Arc IV and give the party a countervoice not aligned to clan, cult, or crusade
- If lost: Preserve her warnings in notes, rites, or a later Warden successor who inherits her concerns without her patience

## The Marked Pilgrim

- Role: Flexible recurring witness tied to a PC's ancestry, oath, or unfinished guilt
- Public face: Refugee, porter, or traveler who should not matter much and therefore matters immediately
- Hidden truth: The Sanguine Knot keeps answering through them, around them, or to what their bloodline remembers
- Wants from the party: Safety, explanation, or release from what is following them
- Best use: Personalize the relic before direct contact
- If lost: Keep their memory active through visions, blood-signs, or spectral return
