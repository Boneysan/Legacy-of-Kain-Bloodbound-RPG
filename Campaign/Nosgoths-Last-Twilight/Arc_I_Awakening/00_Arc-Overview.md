# Arc I: Awakening

## Arc Purpose

Arc I establishes the campaign's tone, teaches the party that every faction lies by omission, and brings the Sanguine Knot into play at Avernus Cathedral and the Blood-Well. It should also plant the campaign's later arguments in small form: truth versus mercy, custody versus purge, and whether revelation without judgment is just another cruelty.

## Level Band

- Recommended levels: 3-5
- Target length: 5-8 sessions

## Core Locations

- Cathedral road and caravan route
- Outer grounds of Avernus Cathedral
- Cult-controlled nave and chapels
- Blood-Well sanctum beneath the cathedral
- First involuntary Spectral overlap zone

## Avernus Causality

- Avernus Cathedral is the present-day cult seat and social trap. This is where false charity, possession, and staged refuge operate.
- The Blood-Well is older than the cult and older than the current political order. It exists because blood-magic was codified here around Janos Audron's stolen-heart legacy.
- The Sanguine Knot is the current Heart-fragment lodged in that older wound. It is what turns ancestry, oath, and hidden inheritance into immediate pressure during the arc.
- Hash'ak'gik corruption does not explain every strange thing below Avernus. Use it for profanation, occupation, and contamination, not as the original cause of the Well.

## Active Factions

- Cult of Hash'ak'gik
- Sarafan Order
- Outlands Guilds
- Pale Accord
- Cult of the Wheel as an indirect funerary and surrender pressure, not yet a full open front
- One Balance-minded observer, Warden trace, or judging voice who notices what the Sanguine Knot is costing
- Kain as distant pressure rather than an active patron

## Session Beats

1. Road encounter, false refuge, and first signs of cult infiltration.
2. Investigation inside or around Avernus, identifying useful allies and compromised NPCs.
3. First descent toward the Blood-Well with pressure from both zealots and scavengers.
4. Contact with the Sanguine Knot and the campaign's first real Corruption choice.
5. Public fallout: who now knows the party touched the Heart, and who wants them contained.

## Questions To Force

- Who in the party trusts holy authority, blood authority, or neither?
- What is each character willing to sacrifice to learn what the fragment knows?
- Which early ally is worth protecting once their compromise is revealed?
- When the party say Avernus, do they mean the cult above, the older blood-site below, or the fragment now awakening inside it?

## Milestone Triggers

- The party secure or decisively lose access to the Sanguine Knot.
- A Vessel of Hash'ak'gik is exposed, escaped, or destroyed.
- The party leave Avernus with a declared ally, declared enemy, and an unmistakable reputation.

## Carry Forward

- Which faction believes the party can still be manipulated.
- Which NPC knows too much about the Blood-Well.
- Whether the Sanguine Knot was hidden, studied, carried, or handed over.
- Whether the Sarafan now see the party as evidence to be contained or corruption to be purged.
- Whether the first fragment use already taught the party that truth can unmake as easily as it reveals.
