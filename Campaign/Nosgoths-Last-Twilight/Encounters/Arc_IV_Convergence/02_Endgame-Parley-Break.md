# Endgame Parley Break

- Arc: Convergence
- Recommended levels: 16-17
- Target TV: 19 Sarafan package, 19 Fracture package, 27-31 reinforced
- Difficulty: Pressure
- Use when: The opening alliance map on the Pillar Heath needs to cost blood, trust, or political capital immediately.

## Location

- Site: Coalition camp, parley ground, or ritual perimeter on the Pillar Heath
- Terrain: Banner lines, temporary wards, witness positions, and exposed negotiation space where one dead envoy can redraw the alliance map
- Pressure: The encounter should make coexistence feel fragile before the party ever reach the Hall of Equilibrium

## Encounter Objective

- Keep the parley from collapsing into irreversible faction war before the party have drawn the coalition lines they can actually live with.

## Running Notes

- Use either the Sarafan break package or the Fracture break package by default.
- The target should be a witness, relic courier, or symbolically important ally, not the sturdiest PC by default.
- Reinforce only if Session 1 must carry a full combat load.
- Even if the party win fast, let the political cost land before the scene ends.
