# Road to the Hall

- Arc: Convergence
- Recommended levels: 16-18
- Target TV: 13 metaphysical package, 13 physical package, 20-24 reinforced
- Difficulty: Pressure
- Use when: The route to the Hall of Equilibrium should test coalition legitimacy, not merely movement speed.

## Location

- Site: Moving judgment-path across the Pillar Heath or the threshold approach to the Hall
- Terrain: Oath-responsive ground, disappearing routes, mirrored dilemmas, and one protected lane that exists only for those willing to pay the right cost
- Pressure: The party must protect the person, rite, or coalition element needed to reach the Hall while the path itself judges prior choices

## Encounter Objective

- Reach the Hall with the required ally, guide, or rite-component intact before the path or the enemy bloc breaks coalition cohesion.

## Running Notes

- Use either the metaphysical package or the physical package by default.
- The metaphysical version should feel like punishment and exposure; the physical version should feel like hostile testing of the line.
- Reinforce only if the road itself is meant to be a full confrontation.
- Log which prior choices the Hall exposes here, because those should matter again in the Black Fulcrum chamber.
