# Purge at First Light

- Arc: Awakening
- Recommended levels: 5-6
- Target TV: 16
- Use when: Sarafan pressure needs to become visible, immediate, and public.

## Location

- Site: Half-burned hamlet on the edge of Sarafan response range
- Terrain: Rooftops, collapsing prayer walls, alleys choked with ash, and innocent bystanders hiding in cellars
- Pressure: The Sarafan will target any vampire, revenant, or visibly corrupted PC first and dare the party to prove the accusation false while civilians are trapped nearby

## Encounter Objective

- Evacuate innocents, survive the purge, or force the commander to confront a false accusation before the village burns.
- Frame the fight as moral pressure in public, not just a duel against two elites.

## Running Notes

- The Inquisitor should read the room, pick the most suspect PC, and try to turn the settlement against them.
- The Clockwork Knight is the hard enforcement piece that makes escape lanes feel temporary.
- If the party wins cleanly, move Sarafan pressure toward investigation and schism rather than immediate second-wave extermination.
