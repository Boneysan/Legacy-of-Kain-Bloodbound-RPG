# The Healer's Smile

- Arc: Awakening
- Recommended levels: 4-5
- Target TV: 15
- Difficulty: Standard
- Use when: Investigation inside Avernus has to break into open horror.

## Location

- Site: Hospice chapel or relief ward inside the Avernus precinct
- Terrain: Narrow aisles, hanging censers, curtained cots, reliquary rooms, and blood channels hidden under the floorboards
- Pressure: The party must expose the wrongness before it escapes into the surrounding refugee population

## Encounter Objective

- Expose the Vessel before it escapes into the refugee camp or completes a possession rite.
- Keep the scene focused on proof, panic, and compromised care rather than a simple room-clear.

## Running Notes

- Let the reveal happen after clues, not as a random monster drop.
- The Vessel opens with social misdirection if possible, then shifts into Voice of the Pit and sacrificial survival tactics once cornered.
- Use crypt stairs or side corridors if the party identifies the suspect early and forces a chase instead of a chapel standoff.
