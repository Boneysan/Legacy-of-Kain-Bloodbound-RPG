# Avernus Exit Pursuit

- Arc: Awakening
- Recommended levels: 5-6
- Target TV: 11 Sarafan version, 8 cult version, 12 with hidden cult leader
- Difficulty: Pressure
- Use when: Arc I should end on pressure, pursuit, and consequence rather than another sealed-chamber fight.

## Location

- Site: Cathedral road out of Avernus, outer sanctuaries, or a ruined checkpoint east of the city
- Terrain: Burned shrines, exhausted mounts, refugee traffic, and too many witnesses for any faction to act without social cost
- Pressure: Whoever catches the party here is trying to define what the Avernus disaster means before the road carries the story elsewhere

## Encounter Objective

- Escape Avernus with the fragment, the witnesses, or the surviving truth intact while the pursuers try to seize, discredit, or publicly brand the party.

## Running Notes

- Use either the Sarafan package or the cult package by default, not both together unless Arc I needs a final spike.
- The Sarafan version is quieter and custody-driven; the cult version is desperate and contamination-driven.
- If V05-C concealment holds and no witnesses survived, skip this encounter entirely. Only trigger it if the hiding place is seen, guessed, or betrayed.
- Let civilian presence matter. A clean tactical victory that burns witness trust should still feel costly.
- If the party already won social control, run this as a short interception or accusation scene rather than a full road battle.
