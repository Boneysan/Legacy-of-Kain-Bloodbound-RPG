# Blood-Well Chamber Defense

- Arc: Awakening
- Recommended levels: 4-6
- Target TV: 16 plus active terrain pressure
- Difficulty: Standard
- Use when: First contact with the Sanguine Knot needs to feel like a split-focus crisis rather than a single straight fight.

## Location

- Site: The Blood-Well chamber beneath Avernus
- Terrain: Profaned ritual stations, blood-fed floor channels, older Accord markings, and shifting cover lines altered by spoken names and vows
- Pressure: The chamber itself is unstable; the party are balancing relic contact, witness safety, and interruption of profanation at the same time

## Encounter Objective

- Hold the chamber together long enough for one meaningful contact with the Knot while preventing the cult from finishing a profanation step or sacrificing a key witness.

## Running Notes

- The Vessel should threaten panic, possession, and ritual momentum more than raw damage output.
- The Acolytes should screen the Vessel, protect ritual stations, and force the party to split attention.
- Each round, let the room change: channels flood, cover opens, or a spectral afterimage reveals a safer or more dangerous lane.
- If the party avoid combat cleanly, keep the same objective structure and replace the roster's pressure with ritual collapse and possession cascade risk.
