# Blood-Channel Hold

- Arc: Awakening
- Recommended levels: 4-5
- Target TV: 12 baseline, 16 with the hired-blade variant
- Difficulty: Pressure
- Use when: The descent beneath Avernus needs a choke-point fight that makes time, secrecy, and rescue matter at once.

## Location

- Site: Flooded antechamber, narrow descent stair, or blood-channel junction beneath Avernus
- Terrain: Slick stone, partial flooding, failing steps, and spectral bleed that can split the front and rear of the party if they overextend
- Pressure: The cult does not need to win cleanly; it only needs to delay the party long enough for a deeper rite, transfer, or withdrawal to complete

## Encounter Objective

- Break the hold before the cult completes its delay action, while still deciding whether captives, witnesses, or secrecy matter more than speed.

## Running Notes

- The Acolytes fight as a delaying knot, not as duelists; they should fall back from one line only to hold the next.
- Treat any compromised guide, retreating handler, or rite-operator as the real clock rather than a separate combatant.
- If you use the Raider, make them proof the lower passages are older and more commercially reused than the cult admits.
- Reward clever movement, silence, and split priorities more than a clean body count.
