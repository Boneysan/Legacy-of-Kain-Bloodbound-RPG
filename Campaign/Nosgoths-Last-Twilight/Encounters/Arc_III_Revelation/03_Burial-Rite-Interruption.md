# Burial Rite Interruption

- Arc: Revelation
- Recommended levels: 11-13
- Target TV: 13 baseline, 23 with the reinforced package
- Difficulty: Pressure
- Use when: The Cult of the Wheel's appeal needs to become mechanically and politically visible without reducing it to simple villain theatrics.

## Location

- Site: Wheel-influenced refuge, mortuary chapel, or controlled mourning hall
- Terrain: Ritual circles, confession alcoves, measured candlelight, and civilians whose panic changes the politics of the room immediately
- Pressure: The party are not just breaking a rite; they are deciding whether calm here is compassion, coercion, or metaphysical theft

## Encounter Objective

- Expose whether the rite is chosen, coerced, or enforced before the Wheel turns grief and exhaustion into one more clean act of surrender.

## Running Notes

- The Mourner should anchor the rite through presence, inevitability, and emotional control rather than brute force.
- The Shades should stay at the edges until the party breaks the social calm or the ritual geometry.
- If the scene should stay lighter, run it as a rescue from ritual quieting and only bring the Shades in when the rite is disrupted.
- Let any civilian panic, confession, or witness collapse matter as much as tactical success.
