# Corridor of Contradictions

- Arc: Revelation
- Recommended levels: 12-14
- Target TV: 19 plus witness pressure
- Difficulty: Pressure
- Use when: A betrayal, manipulated memory, or false narrative needs to resolve under unstable reality rather than in a static conversation.

## Location

- Site: Split-history corridor, archive bridge, or accusation chamber inside the Chronoplast approach
- Terrain: Duplicate doors, phased cover, and history scars that change which path, ally, or enemy is currently real
- Pressure: The encounter should protect a witness or expose a lie while keeping chronology itself from deciding the argument first

## Encounter Objective

- Keep the key witness, betrayer, or contradiction alive and legible long enough for the party to force one version of the truth to stand.

## Running Notes

- The Wraith should embody unstable memory, while the Walker embodies opportunistic rupture.
- Make the witness or betrayer the real center of the encounter; if they die or vanish, the tactical win should feel incomplete.
- If combat is avoided, run the same scene as a collapsing rite of false remembrance that still costs the party a bond or alliance.
- Reward corroboration, positioning, and emotional choices over raw DPR.
