# Frontier Contact Fight

- Arc: Ascension
- Recommended levels: 8-9
- Target TV: 10 baseline, 19 with late-arriving escalation
- Difficulty: Pressure
- Use when: The north needs its first real contact-zone fight, where rescue and orientation matter more than a clean kill count.

## Location

- Site: Northern travel route, compromised outpost, or shattered camp near the Hylden approaches
- Terrain: Glyph-static, weak visibility, uncertain distance, and civilians or scouts who matter more than the enemy body count
- Pressure: This is the scene where the party learn that the north breaks logistics, perception, and trust before it breaks armies

## Encounter Objective

- Rescue survivors, secure proof of Hylden escalation, or hold the route long enough to keep the frontier from collapsing into rumor and panic.

## Running Notes

- The Grunts should feel wrong in motion and loyalty, not merely weaker versions of later Hylden units.
- Use the victim or scavenger to show that ordinary people can become vectors, witnesses, or opportunists under the same pressure.
- Bring in the Shock Trooper only once the party think they understand the scale of the threat.
- Reward evacuation, witness protection, and evidence recovery as much as damage output.
