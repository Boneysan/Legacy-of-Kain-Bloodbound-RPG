# Claimant's Test

- Arc: Ascension
- Recommended levels: 6-8
- Target TV: 18 infiltration route, 14 sabotage route, variable for a duel route
- Difficulty: Pressure
- Use when: The party's chosen method of influencing fortress politics needs to become public standing rather than private competence.

## Location

- Site: Duel gallery, sealed barracks, oath chamber, or inner record hall inside Dumah's Fortress
- Terrain: Oath banners, broken murder-slits, sealed side vaults, and old command architecture that rewards rank, bloodline, or formal challenge
- Pressure: The true stakes are political. The encounter should leave behind a visible answer to who gained standing and why.

## Encounter Objective

- Turn the party's preferred route, whether duel, infiltration, or sabotage exposure, into a result that changes public standing inside the fortress.

## Running Notes

- Use the Skeletal Knight and Thralls for the infiltration route, the Inquisitor and Footsoldiers for the sabotage route, and a clan champion from an existing vampire base for the duel route.
- Do not stack every route at once. Pick the package that best fits the session's politics.
- If the party win privately but cannot prove it publicly, treat the result as incomplete.
- Let witnesses and insult matter as much as damage dealt.
