# The Empty Throne Gate

- Arc: Ascension
- Recommended levels: 6-7
- Target TV: 17-20
- Difficulty: Standard
- Use when: The party first reaches Dumah's Fortress and the gate itself must test their claim.

## Location

- Site: Approach to Dumah's Fortress
- Terrain: Fortress rubble, murder holes, collapsed outer works, and oath-marked gate stones that flare when blood is spilled
- Pressure: The scene should force the party to decide whether the fortress is spoil, shrine, or responsibility before any claimant welcomes them inside

## Monster Roster

| Creature | Count | TV Each | Total TV | Monster Source |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| Outcast Raider | 2 | 4 | 8 | Monster_Manual/03_Mortals-and-Cultists.md |
| Spellbound Golem | 1 | 5 | 5 | Monster_Manual/05_Constructs-and-Automatons.md |
| Flex elite rival | 1 | 4-7 | 4-7 | Choose the claimant's champion or another local elite that fits the current fortress politics |

## Encounter Objective

- Take the gate, hold the gate, or refuse the claim publicly.
- Use the flex slot to decide which faction is testing the party first rather than treating the scene as generic wilderness combat.

## Running Notes

- Keep the Raiders as opportunists exploiting confusion, not as the true center of the scene.
- The Golem represents older warded authority that does not care who thinks they own the fortress now.
- If you want a cleaner baseline, set the flex elite at TV 5 so the encounter sits at TV 18.
