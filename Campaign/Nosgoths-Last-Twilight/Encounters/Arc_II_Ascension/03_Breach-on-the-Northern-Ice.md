# Breach on the Northern Ice

- Arc: Ascension
- Recommended levels: 7-9
- Target TV: 18 baseline before terrain pressure
- Difficulty: Pressure
- Use when: The northern crisis needs a clean first proof that the Hylden front is real.

## Location

- Site: Breach zone near the Hylden frontier
- Terrain: Ice shelves, collapsing rift edges, glyph-static, and unstable pylons that can be overloaded with a DR 3 Glyphcasting or Craft check
- Pressure: Normal coordination fails; the battlefield itself teaches the party that containment is a technical and metaphysical problem, not only a military one

## Monster Roster

| Creature | Count | TV Each | Total TV | Monster Source |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| Hylden Shock Trooper | 2 | 9 | 18 | Monster_Manual/06_Hylden-Forces.md |

## Encounter Objective

- Shut the breach, save the possessed, or retreat with proof of Hylden escalation.

## Running Notes

- Run the Shock Troopers as a telepathic pair rather than two separate bruisers.
- Use the terrain to isolate PCs and threaten Soul Override on downed bodies.
- If the party solve the node problem intelligently, let that matter more than killing both Troopers.
