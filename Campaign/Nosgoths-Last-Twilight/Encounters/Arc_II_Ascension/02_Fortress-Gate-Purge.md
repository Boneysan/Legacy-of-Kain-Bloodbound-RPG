# Fortress Gate Purge

- Arc: Ascension
- Recommended levels: 6-10
- Target TV: 25 baseline, 33 for the heavier version
- Difficulty: Challenging
- Use when: Sarafan pressure turns the fortress dispute into a public custody-or-purge crisis.

## Location

- Site: Gate tunnel and inner threshold of Dumah's Fortress
- Terrain: Murder-slit gallery, oath banners granting half cover near the portcullis crank, and a winch chain that can be cut with a DR 3 Blood or Fury check to drop the inner gate
- Pressure: The Sarafan want one visible failure they can turn into doctrine and public justification

## Encounter Objective

- Hold the claimant, witness, or command sigil for 3 rounds, or escort them through the inner gate before the Sarafan can brand the whole fortress faction as irredeemable.

## Trigger Conditions

- Fire this encounter when Sarafan hardliners can plausibly justify intervention at the threshold through public endorsement, exposure, or institutional collapse.
- Most common triggers: V01-A public Turel endorsement, V02-D public exposure of the loyalty task, or V01-D institutional cover collapse.

## Running Notes

- Footsoldiers lock the threshold and exploit formation pressure.
- The Outrider hunts escape routes and isolated or visibly corrupted targets.
- The Inquisitor decides who counts as lawful custody and who counts as cleansing fuel.
- For higher-level parties, add a second Inquisitor instead of inflating the existing unit behavior.
