# Iron Echo Chamber Crisis

- Arc: Ascension
- Recommended levels: 7-9
- Target TV: 26 baseline before claimant or witness complications
- Difficulty: Standard
- Use when: The Iron Echo begins answering and the party must decide whether legitimacy is granted, exposed, refused, or weaponized.

## Location

- Site: The Iron Echo chamber, command dais, and relic access hall beneath Dumah's Fortress
- Terrain: Oathstones, awakened ward lines, visible spectral banners, and a ruling dais that reacts to blood, challenge, and lineage
- Pressure: The fragment is forcing a ruling under unstable political conditions; a clean tactical win that leaves legitimacy unresolved should count as incomplete

## Encounter Objective

- Reach a binding answer about who the Echo recognizes before the chamber is seized, desecrated, or turned into proof that no claimant can actually command what Dumah built.

## Running Notes

- The undead garrison should feel like command pressure made visible, not random tomb monsters.
- Add claimant champions, rival retainers, or a saboteur only if the scene needs a second political blade, not extra bulk.
- Let the chamber answer blood, oath, and challenge in real time so the terrain itself judges the party's method.
- If combat is avoided, keep the same objective and resolve it through trial, public humiliation, or forced recognition under relic pressure.
