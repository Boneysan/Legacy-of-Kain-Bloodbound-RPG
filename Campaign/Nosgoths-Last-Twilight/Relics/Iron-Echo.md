# The Iron Echo

## Site

Dumah's Fortress

## Associated Pressure

- Pillar pressure: Conflict
- Arc: Ascension

## First Impression

The fragment rings without being struck. In its presence, old commands return, war-codes sharpen, and every claim of martial legitimacy starts to sound like destiny.

## Primary Claimants

- Turelim and Dumahim claimants
- Blood Knights
- Sarafan war leaders
- Any PC who believes rule can be earned by combat

## Contact Cost

- Base test: DR 3 Will or Fury
- Failure: 1 Corruption and a compulsion to answer insult with violence or formal challenge
- Deliberate use: 1-2 Corruption, plus potential blood-debt consequences

## Practical Uses

- Awaken fortress wards or martial memory locks
- Bind a vow of service, challenge, or temporary truce
- Reveal who broke a military oath in the fortress's past
- Impose order on a crisis without guaranteeing that the resulting command is just

## Regional Fallout

- Duels proliferate
- Weak command structures collapse into bloodshed
- Soldiers become harder to calm once battle starts
- The appearance of legitimate rule may harden even when the underlying settlement is morally false
- Balance pressure increases if the fragment is used to enforce obedience without resolving the deeper claim
