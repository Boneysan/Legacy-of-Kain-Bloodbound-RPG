# The Black Fulcrum

## Site

Pillar Heath and the Hall of Equilibrium

## Associated Pressure

- Pillar pressure: Balance
- Arc: Convergence

## First Impression

The fragment feels less like an object than a verdict. It weighs every oath, every compromise, and every previous fragment use as if the campaign itself has become evidence.

Nothing reaches it clean. If the party arrive believing they can solve the endgame without being judged for how they got here, the Fulcrum should prove them wrong immediately.

## Primary Claimants

- Every surviving faction
- Wardens of Balance
- Kain
- Any PC who believes they have earned the right to decide for Nosgoth

## Contact Cost

- Base test: DR 4 Will or Soul
- Failure: 1-2 Corruption and a severe revelation about the self, the party, or the campaign's true cost
- Deliberate use: 2-3 Corruption, plus immediate regional instability if mishandled

## Practical Uses

- Complete, seal, break, or redirect the assembled Heart
- Stabilize or destabilize the final rite at the Pillar Heath
- Decide whether the Heart's final use reinforces failing seals around the Hylden breach network, substitutes for a lost stabilizing structure at catastrophic cost, or tears the boundary wider in exchange for anti-Wheel leverage
- Force the final coalition to reveal its real priorities

## Regional Fallout

- Pillar effects flare across Nosgoth
- Spectral and Material boundaries threaten to collapse locally
- The campaign end-state becomes irreversible within scenes, not sessions
- The final state of the Hylden wound and the Wheel's hold become visibly linked to the party's chosen answer
- Balance is revealed not as neutrality but as the cost-bearing structure that survives after every faction has called its appetite necessity
