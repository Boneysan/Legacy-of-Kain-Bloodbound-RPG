## Shared Relic Rules

- First contact should reveal truth and danger simultaneously.
- Every fragment should tempt action before it grants understanding.
- Every fragment should create faction movement within one session of being revealed.
- Corruption from a fragment should alter both the bearer and the region around it.
- Every fragment should also leave a Balance consequence, even when the immediate outcome looks like success.
- When the Sarafan react to a fragment, decide whether the legalists or the Hollow hardliners are gaining authority from the situation.
- When instability spreads, decide whether the Crimson Fracture gains recruits, proof, or cover from the fallout.
- Use the Faction-Tracker to record who benefited, who was humiliated, and what metaphysical debt the fragment use created.
