# The Rift Ember

## Site

Near the Hylden Gate and the Resonance Pillar

## Associated Pressure

- Pillar pressure: Dimension / Energy
- Arc: Ascension

## First Impression

The fragment burns cold and hums in geometric pulses. Space folds around it, glyphs answer it, and every barrier in the region begins to look temporary.

## Primary Claimants

- Hylden Remnants
- Glyphwrights
- Pale Accord chapters
- Desperate commanders who think any seal can be weaponized

## Contact Cost

- Base test: DR 3 Focus or Soul
- Failure: 1 Corruption and short-term spatial distortion or lost time
- Deliberate use: 2-3 Corruption when powering breaches, engines, or gates

## Practical Uses

- Power or shut down Hylden devices
- Force open a narrow planar breach
- Stabilize or sabotage a glyph lattice temporarily
- Temporarily reinforce a failing seal, replace a broken stabilizing structure, or widen the wound for strategic gain

## Regional Fallout

- Spectral bleed worsens
- Distances become unreliable
- Hylden possession attempts and glyph-static intensify
- Hylden negotiators gain leverage wherever survival and compromise become harder to distinguish
- Crimson Fracture rhetoric spreads whenever people conclude the wound is more honest than the old border
- Any apparent success here should leave a visible question: was the breach healed, merely delayed, or taught a new shape?
