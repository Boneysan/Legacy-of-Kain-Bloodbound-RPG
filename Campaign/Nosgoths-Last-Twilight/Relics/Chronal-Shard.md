# The Chronal Shard

## Site

The Chronoplast

## Associated Pressure

- Pillar pressure: Time / Mind
- Arc: Revelation

## First Impression

The fragment never appears the same way twice. It reflects futures that did not happen, memories that might have, and conversations one participant does not yet remember.

## Primary Claimants

- Kain
- Moebian loyalists or archive thieves
- Cult of the Wheel
- PCs obsessed with proof, fate, or revision

## Contact Cost

- Base test: DR 3 Will or Focus
- Failure: 1 Corruption and one false memory accepted as true until challenged in play
- Deliberate use: 2 Corruption, with a risk of paradox fallout or lost time

## Practical Uses

- Reveal two or more possible outcomes of a major decision
- Identify where a prophecy was manipulated
- Locate time-scarred paths, hidden archive states, or erased betrayals
- Confirm that an impossible witness, record, or wound is real, while still changing what it means

## Regional Fallout

- NPCs remember incompatible versions of events
- Timelines overlap in dreams and ruins
- The party may become harder to predict and harder to trust
- The Cult of the Wheel gains new ways to curate surrender through prophecy, while the Fracture gains proof that contradiction is freedom
- Any truth recovered here should create at least one new enemy who preferred uncertainty to clarified history
